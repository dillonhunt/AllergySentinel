Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone ‚Üí Tap your name at the top ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Select Allergy Sentinel ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Allergy Sentinel ‚Üí Go to Settings (gear icon) ‚Üí Tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Why isn't the app showing data for my location?

Ensure location services are enabled for Allergy Sentinel in Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Location Services. For best results, select "While Using the App" or "Always".

How accurate is the pollen data?

We use hyperlocal data with 1km precision from leading environmental data providers including Breezometer. Data is updated multiple times per hour for real-time accuracy.

Is my health data private?

Yes. All your symptom logs, allergy profile, and personal settings are stored entirely on your device. We never upload your health data to our servers.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find your Allergy Sentinel purchase, and select "Request a refund".
